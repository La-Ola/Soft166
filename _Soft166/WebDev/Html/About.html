The Game

This web game is Naughts and Crosses.

The game is set on a 3 by 3 grid, and is designed for two players.
The tiles assigned to each player is a naught "O"
and a cross "X". Player one will always be crosses
and player two will always be naughts.
Crosses go first.

The final aim of the game is to use tactics, and out-smart
your opponent by getting three of your tiles in a row of any
given direction. This could be any of the three vertical,
horizontal, or the two diagonals.

First player to five, wins.

The Lights

Each player gets to choose one of the 4 available colours for
their lights. They cannot have the same colour. Player one
is assigned lights 1, 2, and 3; Player two is assigned lights
4, 5, and 6.

As it comes to each players turn, their assigned lights will
glow brighter. The winner will have a paraded light show of
their colour displayed on all six lights upon completion
of the game.
